How this tracker works
Watchlist tab: Your 6 personal routes. Hit "Scan prices" and it checks each one on Google Flights via Serpapi, logging the result.
Price history tab: Every scan is saved locally in your browser. Over time this builds a baseline — the app learns what "normal" costs for each route.
Deal detection: Once you have ~10 data points per route, the app flags prices that are more than 20% below your personal average as a deal.
Budget: Each full scan uses 6 of your 250 monthly Serpapi searches. Scanning every other day = ~90 searches/month, well within the free tier.
GitHub later: When you're ready, this same logic moves to a GitHub Action that runs automatically every morning — no file to open.
